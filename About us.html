About Sierra Leone Football Club

Pride of the Nation Since 1985

Our History

Sierra Leone Football Club was founded in 1985 with a vision to create a football institution that would represent the passion and talent of Sierra Leone on both national and international stages. What started as a small local club has grown into a powerhouse of African football.

Over the decades, we have won 15 major trophies, produced 12 international players, and become a symbol of sporting excellence in West Africa. Our journey is marked by resilience, community spirit, and an unwavering commitment to beautiful football.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To develop and promote football excellence while fostering national pride, youth development, and community engagement through the beautiful game.

Vision

To become the leading football club in Africa, recognized for sporting excellence, youth development, and positive community impact.

Sierra Leone Football Agency

Our Role & Responsibilities

As the premier football institution in Sierra Leone, our agency duties include:

Youth Development

Operating one of West Africa's most successful youth academies, identifying and nurturing local talent from age 8.

Player Representation

Managing professional contracts and career development for Sierra Leonean players both locally and internationally.

International Relations

Facilitating player transfers, international partnerships, and football diplomacy with clubs worldwide.

Community Engagement

Running football programs for underprivileged youth and promoting sports as a tool for social development.
